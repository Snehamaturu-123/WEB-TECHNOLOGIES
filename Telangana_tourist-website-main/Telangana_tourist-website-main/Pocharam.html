About

Steeped in history and natural beauty, Pocharam Wildlife Sanctuary offers more than just a scenic escape. It's also a place where legends intertwine with the present. The sanctuary's ancient temples and ruins stand as testaments to its rich cultural heritage, while its tranquil lakes and meandering trails beckon adventurers to explore its hidden treasures. Whether you're seeking solitude in nature or eager to uncover the mysteries of the past, Pocharam Wildlife Sanctuary promises an unforgettable journey filled with discovery and wonder.
